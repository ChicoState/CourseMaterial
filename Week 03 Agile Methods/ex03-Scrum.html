1. Identify who in your team will serve as Product Owner and who will serve as Scrum Master. Remember: these cannot be the same person.
  2. Decide what tool(s) you will use to keep track of your Product and Sprint Backlogs as well as the Burndown Chart. The Product Owner should set it up.
  3. The Product Owner (only!) should submit below:

    • Lab time (Noon, 1pm, 4pm)
    • Team Members (full names) with Roles (and make note of who is absent)
    • What tool(s) you are using for #2 above, with a link (if applicable)
  4. Start on your Sprint!
